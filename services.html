What we offer?

Come take a closer look. Prototype media is anchored on five subsidiaries through which we deliver our services
Introducing PRO-CREATIVES
This forms the core of Prototype Media as its the content creative arm of our company. This is where we transform ideas into reality. Our deliverables include;
Idea generation
Voice Overs
Animation
Copy writing
Graphic design
Audio production
Visual
Introducing PROTO TV
This is an online visual information sharing arm of Prototype Media that aims at broadcasting information in form of news bulletins, documentaries, histories, short stories, etc.
The Communicator Academy
This is the training arm of Prototype Media LTD aimed at training young passionate minds and equipping them with skills in broadcasting, public speaking, leadership, values and practical media skills that will aid them in societal development.
Introducing PROTO RADIO
Prototype Media is aiming to establish two radio stations: Pro FM - A commercial business talk radio station, the first of its kind in Nigeria, aimed at promoting businesses. Zone FM - A commercial inspirational Music and lifestyle radio station promoting good societal values.
